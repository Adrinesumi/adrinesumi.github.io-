E-Bookery: Read Through Life

In a chaotic information overloaded environment, reading a book while tucked up in your bed or sitting under a tree or in a quiet library is no less than meditation! The reason is that while reading a book every person interprets and imagines things as they see them, whereas in a movie everything that you see is entirely the director’s interpretation! Every dark street, or alley, or tree described in a story is owned by you and only you see it that way – which is uniquely different. Presenting you ‘E Bookery International’ - a reading group specifically for readers who read through life, intending to bridge the global distance and share literature through storytelling. With an aim to promote a love of literature; learn and discuss something that matters to them, E Bookery International invites participants to bring up suggestions, formulate opinions, and discuss stories from a wide array of genres.

Once a week, our participants get an opportunity to analyze the content of any book in form of book reviews, form their thoughts, and present them in public as storytelling. With a demographically diverse audience, participants are encouraged to push themselves and grow as readers and learners through our practice, something that can be applied to other areas of their day-to-day lives as well. Two voluntary speakers every week will be sharing or reviewing any literature of their choice for twenty minutes each. With an aim to take you on a journey from being a member of E Bookery International to providing you a chance to publish your book – we wish to expand and grow into a community of avid readers and leaders.
